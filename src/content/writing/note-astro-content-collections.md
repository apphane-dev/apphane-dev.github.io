> **This is a seed example post.** It shows how a *note* looks and reads, and can
> be deleted once real notes accumulate.

A note is working knowledge, not a finished essay. It is allowed to be short, to
grow, and to show its age. This one carries a **growing** status and an
`updatedAt` date so no one mistakes it for a fixed article.

## What a note keeps

- The `glob` loader lives in `astro/loaders` and takes a `base` and a `pattern`.
- `reference('authors')` validates that a post points at an author that exists.
- `z.coerce.date()` lets frontmatter hold a bare `YYYY-MM-DD` string.

## Why keep notes at all

Because the fact you looked up twice is the fact you'll look up a third time. A
note that is useful in its current state — even three bullets — is worth
publishing. Maturity is shown, never assumed.
